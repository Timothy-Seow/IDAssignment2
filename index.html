League of Legends

League of Legends is a 5v5 team-based strategy game where each player picks a Champion to play and fight to victory by destroying the opponent's base. Over 100 Champions are available for you to choose, each having their own unique abiltiies and play style, increasing the game difficulty and making it more versatile for players.

The Developers

League of Legends is made by Riot Games, a American company founded by Marc Merrill (Left) and Bradon Beck (Center) in 2006. It is currently led by CEO Nicolo Laurent (Right) and headquarted in Los Angeles, California, with over 3000 employees and over 20 offices worldwide.
